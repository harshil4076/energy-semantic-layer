# Portfolio Sustainability Goals 2017

## Headline targets

- **20% reduction** in portfolio-wide EUI by end of 2018 vs 2016 baseline
- **Zero critical HVAC work orders** open for more than 30 days
- All buildings in the **top quartile** of their peer group (by primaryspaceusage) for electricity EUI

## Site-level targets

| Site     | Target EUI (kWh/sqft/yr) | Current status |
|----------|--------------------------|----------------|
| Bull     | ≤ 18.0                   | At risk        |
| Cockatoo | ≤ 15.0                   | On track       |
| Hog      | ≤ 14.0                   | On track       |
| Bear     | ≤ 16.0                   | At risk        |
| Panther  | ≤ 17.0                   | On track       |
| Robin    | ≤ 13.5                   | Exceeded       |

## Priority actions

1. Address all open Critical and High priority HVAC work orders before Q3 2017
2. Complete LED retrofit program across all education buildings by mid-2017
3. Implement overnight BAS setback schedules on all sites where not active
4. Review steam trap condition across all steam-metered buildings annually

## Reporting cadence

- Monthly EUI report to facilities leadership (first Monday of month)
- Quarterly sustainability scorecard to executive team
- Annual third-party energy audit for buildings exceeding target EUI by > 20%

# Building Operations Notes

## Bull site — Education (large campus)

- Largest building in the portfolio by sqft (216,050). EUI performance is a portfolio priority.
- Chiller plant was upgraded in 2014. Steam distribution is original (1970s) — multiple trap failures expected.
- BAS is Siemens Desigo. Integration with energy dashboard completed January 2016.
- Known issue: east wing VAV boxes have intermittent communication failures. Monitoring closely.
- Sustainability note: Bull is the highest absolute energy consumer in the portfolio. A 10% reduction here equals the full annual consumption of the Gator site.

## Cockatoo site — Mixed (education + residential)

- Second largest site. Education building (322,341 sqft) is the main driver of electricity consumption.
- District steam served from central plant — metered at building boundary.
- LED retrofit completed for all classrooms Q4 2016. Corridor and parking still on fluorescent.
- Residential lodging buildings have variable occupancy — summer consumption drops ~40%.

## Hog site — Office

- Mid-size office campus (121,714 sqft). Primarily 9–18:00 weekday occupancy.
- Good performance historically. Main risk: server room growth — IT requesting 30% more rack space in 2017.
- Power factor correction installed 2015 — no utility penalty charges since.
- Chilled water plant is most efficient in portfolio (COP ~5.8 at design conditions).

## Bear site — Mixed (education + public services)

- Bear education building (117,814 sqft) has been flagged for above-target EUI two years running.
- Root cause suspected: steam trap failures + lack of overnight setback. Work orders in progress.
- Public services building has unusual load profile — 24/7 operations (emergency services).

## Panther site — Mixed (education + residential + parking)

- Large parking structure dominates sqft but has very low energy intensity. Lighting controls installed 2016.
- Education buildings performing well. Residential (lodging) buildings have high hot water consumption.
- Known anomaly: Panther_lodging_Else had a water heater failure in March 2016 — hotwater spike visible in data.

## Robin site — Education

- Best EUI performer in the education peer group. LEED Gold certified (2012).
- Full BAS with demand-responsive controls. Economizer hours are highest in portfolio.
- Used as the benchmark building for education peer comparisons.
